# Technical Deep Dive

This article lives in the `_articles` collection. By using collections, we separate "evergreen" content from chronological blog posts.

## Why this structure?
* **Organization:** Keep your root directory clean.
* **SEO:** Clear URL structures like `/articles/placeholder-article/`.
